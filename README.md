# Superstore Retail Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail performance using the Superstore dataset to uncover insights across sales, profit, customer segments, regions, and operational metrics. The goal is to support data-driven business decisions by identifying top-performing products and regions, profitability drivers, and opportunities for operational improvement.

The dashboard was built using Power BI, focusing on KPI tracking, trend analysis, and geographic performance visualization.

## Business Questions Answered
- Which regions, states, and cities generate the highest sales and profit?
- Which product categories and sub-categories drive revenue?
- How do different customer segments contribute to overall sales?
- What payment and shipping modes are most commonly used?
- How do sales and profits trend month-over-month year-over-year (YoY)?
- Where are operational inefficiencies such as returns and delivery delays occurring?

## Dashboard Highlights
- Sales and Profit by Region and State using interactive maps
- Category and Sub-Category performance analysis
- Customer Segment analysis
- Payment Mode distribution
- Top 10 States by Sales and Profit

- Shipping Mode analysis

The dashboard supports dynamic filtering by region for Central, East, South, and West markets.

## Key Insights
- Technology and Office Supplies contribute the highest share of sales
- California and New York lead in both sales and profit
- Consumer segment accounts for nearly half of total sales
- Cash on Delivery remains the dominant payment method
- Standard Class is the most frequently used shipping mode
- Seasonal patterns are visible in sales and profit trends
